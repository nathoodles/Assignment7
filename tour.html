Use these cards to guide you move around the structure

The viewer is required to concentrate as they climb the stairs as the normal ratio of the stairs has been changed, additionally, the dip in the center of the stairs throws our vision off as we transfer between going up and down. This forces you to climb slower.

Standing on top of the structure we are supposed to feel like spectators in a stadium, watching the foot traffic and the activities on the field.

The underneath of the structure is lit at night with a white tint, and is designed to resemble the outline of snow capped mountains. Standing underneath the stairs is supposed to remind us of the concealed movement underneath stadiums.
